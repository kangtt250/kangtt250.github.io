# Seon Zhu

> Builder of intelligent systems across finance, AI, and human coordination.

Over the past decade, work has moved across traditional finance, crypto markets, quantitative trading, and financial infrastructure — exploring how technology reshapes capital, coordination, and human systems.

Background includes roles at Binance, Bybit, Ant Group, Gate.io, and leading crypto trading firms, spanning derivatives, institutional business development, trading systems, product strategy, and market structure.

Particular focus is placed on how artificial intelligence reshapes cognition, markets, and civilization — and how individuals can use AI to extend creativity, decision-making, and leverage.

At Rock Bund Capital, current work centers on strategic partnerships, systematic trading expansion, and next-generation infrastructure connecting traditional finance, crypto networks, and machine intelligence.

Ongoing exploration includes philosophy, systems thinking, spirituality, music, and digital culture.

Thinking is deeply influenced by long-term technological cycles, human behavioral systems, and the evolving relationship between intelligence and capital.
